Audio Books

The Bradecote & Catchpoll audiobooks are published by Isis Audiobooks , and are read by Audible Award nominated voice actor Matt Addis. Matt brings the stories to life and makes listening an immersive experience. He is a highly acclaimed voice artist as well as having had major roles on stage, such as in the National Theatre's production of War Horse, with which he toured in 2014/15.

Servant of Death

Matt Addis, award nominated audiobook voice artist, gives a brilliant rendition of the first Bradecote & Catchpoll novel, with a cast that sounds so varied it could be a radio play. Hear, in CD/MP3CD, from iTunes and Audible download formats, how Bradecote and Catchpoll are pushed together in an uneasy alliance to find out who killed Eudo, the lord Bishop of Winchester's clerk and spy.

Listen to Matt reading an extract of the audiobook.

Ordeal by Fire

Available in CD/MP3CD, from iTunes and Audible download formats, Matt Addis brings the words to life off the page, and gives voice to the detecting duo, and of course their new addition, ‘serjeanting apprentice’ Walkelin, with his varied voices and beautifully paced narration. 

Listen to Matt reading an extract of the audiobook.

Marked to Die

The audiobook of Marked to Die, yet again read by the superb Matt Addis, is available from Isis Audiobooks as both CD and CD/MP3 here and is also available for download from Auidble. You can listen to a sample.

Hostage to Fortune

The audiobook of the fourth Bradecote & Catchpoll, Hostage to Fortune, read by 'the voice of Bradectote & Catchpoll', Matt Addis, is available from Isis Audiobooks as both CD and CD/MP3 and is also available as a download from Audible.
