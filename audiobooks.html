Audio Books

The Bradecote & Catchpoll audiobooks are published by Isis Audiobooks at The Reading House and Audible. They are read by Audible Award nominated voice actor Matt Addis. Matt brings the stories to life and makes listening an immersive experience. He is a highly acclaimed voice artist as well as having had major roles on stage, such as in the National Theatre's production of War Horse, with which he toured in 2014/15.
