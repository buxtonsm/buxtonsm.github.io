Servant of Death

Matt Addis, award nominated audiobook voice artist, gives a brilliant rendition of the first Bradecote & Catchpoll novel, with a cast that sounds so varied it could be a radio play. Hear, in CD/MP3CD, from iTunes and Audible download formats, how Bradecote and Catchpoll are pushed together in an uneasy alliance to find out who killed Eudo, the lord Bishop of Winchester's clerk and spy.
